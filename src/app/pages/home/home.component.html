National Education Society for Tribal Students (NESTS)

NESTS has been established with an objective to impart quality education to Scheduled Tribe (ST) children through establishment of Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS). EMRSs provide free education to ST children from Class VI to XII in a residential setup.
